At Bell School, our commitment is to help every child realize their full potential through a curriculum that prioritizes conceptual understanding and real-world application. By blending traditional values with modern teaching methodologies, we challenge and excite young minds from Kindergarten to Grade 12. We embrace a holistic approach that ensures academic excellence is matched by the development of strong moral character and physical vitality.

Answers for
The Questions

We offer a wide variety of activities including sports, arts, music, coding clubs, and leadership programs to ensure holistic development.

Parents can join our active Parent-Teacher Association, volunteer for school events, or participate in our regular open house workshops.

Our ratio is approximately 22 : 1, with 1 teacher and 2 teaching assistants for every class, ensuring personalized attention for each student.

We provide nutritious, balanced hot meals prepared daily by our on-site chefs, catering to various dietary requirements and allergies.

Formal parent-teacher meetings are held at the end of every term, though parents can request a meeting with teachers at any point during the academic year.
